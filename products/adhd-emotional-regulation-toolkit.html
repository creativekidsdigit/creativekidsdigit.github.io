Frequently asked questions

How is this different from the other two toolkits?

The Homework and Morning toolkits focus on routines and tasks. This one focuses on emotions — naming feelings, calming the body, building coping skills. They complement each other perfectly (bundle all 3 for $22).

Is this for the child or the parent?

Both. Pages 3–7 and 11 are kid-facing tools. Pages 8–9 and 12 are parent guides. You use them together — that's what makes this unique.

Can therapists or school counselors use this?

Yes! Many therapists, school counselors, and occupational therapists use these pages in sessions. The calm-down cards and feelings wheel work beautifully in a clinical or school setting.

What age range works best?

Designed for ages 6–12. Younger kids (6–8) benefit most from the feelings wheel, body scan, and calm-down cards. Older kids (9–12) engage more with the emotion tracker and affirmation cards.

Can I cut the calm-down cards apart?

Yes — Pages 5 and 11 are specifically designed to be cut apart. Print on cardstock for durability, or laminate for daily handling.

Does my child need a diagnosis?

No. Any child who struggles with big emotions, emotional impulsivity, or meltdowns will benefit — diagnosed or not.
