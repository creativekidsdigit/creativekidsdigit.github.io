The core method

The 5-Block Homework Routine

ADHD brains crave predictability. Same routine, same order, every day — that’s the magic.

🍎 Reset 10 min — snack, water, movement
→
💛 Connect 5 min — one question, no homework talk
→
📋 Plan 5 min — list tasks, choose order together
→
✏️ Work 15–25 min — one task, timer running
→
🎉 Reward 10 min — celebrate effort, earned break

Frequently asked questions

What age range is this for?

The toolkit is designed for parents of children ages 6–12 with ADHD (or ADHD-like challenges). The strategies work across this range, though you may adjust the reward system for older vs. younger children.

Does my child need a formal ADHD diagnosis?

No. If your child struggles with focus, task initiation, emotional regulation, or homework resistance, this toolkit will help — diagnosis or not. Many parents use it while waiting for assessment.

What format is the download?

You’ll receive a high-resolution PDF (300 DPI, US Letter 8.5 × 11 in). Print at home on any printer, or send to a local print shop for a premium feel.

Can I print it multiple times?

Yes! Print as many copies as you need for your own family. The worksheets are designed to be reprinted weekly (especially the routine planner and reward tracker).

Is this a physical product that ships?

No — this is a digital download. After purchase, you’ll receive an instant download link. No shipping, no waiting. Use it tonight.

What if it doesn’t work for my child?

Every child is different. The toolkit gives you multiple strategies (6 focus techniques, 24 brain breaks, 3 reward tiers) so you can test and find what clicks for your family. Most parents see improvement within the first week.

Can teachers or tutors use this?

Absolutely. The strategies apply in any homework-support setting. Many tutors, learning support assistants, and homeschool parents use it alongside their teaching materials.
