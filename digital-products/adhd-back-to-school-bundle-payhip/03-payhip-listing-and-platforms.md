## 3. AMAZON KDP — interior adaptation notes

The bundle becomes a **printed paperback workbook** on KDP — a Phase 2 move that drives passive royalties and brand authority. It's not a replacement for Payhip; it complements it.

### 3.1 Pick the KDP variant

Two options. Ship one, not both, in the first quarter:

| Variant | Format | Pages | Audience | Recommended? |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **A. ADHD Back-to-School Workbook for Kids** | 8.5×11 paperback, B&W interior, color cover | ~120 | Parent buys for the child | ✓ Recommended first |
| **B. ADHD Back-to-School Parent Companion** | 6×9 paperback, B&W interior, color cover | ~140 | Parent reads alongside the printable | Phase 3 |

> Why A first: visual workbooks convert better in the "Parenting > ADHD" KDP category than text-heavy companions, and the B&W cost is lower at 8.5×11 with high page count.

### 3.2 KDP interior spec (Variant A)

| Spec | Value |
|---|---|
| Trim size | 8.5 × 11 in |
| Page count | 100–120 |
| Interior | Black-and-white |
| Paper | White (60# preferred) |
| Bleed | No bleed (KDP discourages bleeds at this trim) |
| Inner margin (gutter) | 0.5 in |
| Outer margin | 0.5 in |
| Top/bottom margin | 0.5 in |
| Font | Same as digital (Inter + Fraunces). Embed all fonts. |
| File format | Print-ready PDF (X-1a:2001) |

### 3.3 What changes from the digital bundle

- **Color → B&W.** Replace tinted callouts with hatched/dotted patterns. Replace colored backgrounds with light-gray fills (5–10%).
- **One-sided → two-sided.** Reorder pages so each spread "reads" — instructions on the left, fillable on the right.
- **Single use → 30 fillable copies.** Multiply each fillable page so the buyer gets 30 copies of the morning chart, 30 weeks of the planner, etc. This is what makes a 14-page digital → 100+-page paperback.
- **Page numbers + table of contents** added.
- **Cover redesigned for KDP**: spine width based on page count; matte preferred.
- **No live links** in the interior. Replace with the URL spelled out.

### 3.4 Page-by-page adaptation guide

| Digital page | KDP adaptation | Multiplier |
|---|---|---|
| Cover + How-to-Use | Front matter + intro chapter (4 pages) | 1× |

**Total KDP page count:** ~120 pages (within the sweet spot for KDP B&W margin economics).

### 3.7 What KDP will NOT replace

- Payhip stays the primary. KDP royalty per book ($3–$4) is much lower than Payhip net per bundle (~$22 at standard price).
- KDP is a long-tail SEO surface: it shows up in Amazon's "ADHD" search, drives discoverability, and builds brand authority.
- Don't price your KDP paperback below $13.99 — that's the cost floor before margins go negative.
