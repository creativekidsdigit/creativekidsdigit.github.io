# ADHD Back-to-School Bundle — Payhip-First Launch System

A complete, Payhip-first digital-product launch for ADHD families preparing for the school year. Eleven printables in one bundle, designed for fast solo-creator delivery and direct-to-checkout monetization through Payhip (with Shopify and Amazon KDP as secondary surfaces).

**Constraint:** No Etsy. Payhip is the primary store. Pinterest is the primary cold-traffic engine.

**Target buyer:** Parents of ADHD kids ages 6–16 who want one system that handles the school year — not a stack of disconnected charts.

## Headline numbers

- **Bundle:** 11 printables, 14 pages, single PDF, $17 launch / $24 standard / $29 holiday
- **Order bumps:** Editable Canva master ($+9), KDP-formatted version ($+0 — bonus to drive reviews)
- **Mini products:** Each printable also sold standalone at $3–$5 to feed the bundle
- **Pinterest:** 28 unique pins across 14 launch days; problem/solution hooks; 60%+ of net-new buyers expected from this channel
- **Solo-creator build budget:** 12 hours content + 3 hours assets + 14-day launch operations
- **Steady-state maintenance:** ~3 hours/week after Day 14
