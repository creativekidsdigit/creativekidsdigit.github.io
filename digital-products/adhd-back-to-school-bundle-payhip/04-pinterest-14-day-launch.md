# 04 — Pinterest 14-Day Launch Plan (28 Pins)

**Doc purpose:** A complete 14-day Pinterest launch calendar with 2 pins per day (28 pins total), keyword targeting, problem/solution hooks, descriptions, and CTAs that route traffic to Payhip.

**Goal:** Drive cold-traffic discovery + email opt-ins + bundle sales over the first 14 days of launch.

---

## 1. Strategic principles

1. **Every pin solves one specific problem.** Vague pins die. Pin titles read like a parent's exact thought at 9pm on a school night.
2. **Pins route to a blog post or lead magnet first**, not directly to Payhip checkout. Pinterest down-ranks direct-checkout pins.
3. **Every pin has a UTM tag** so we can see which hook → which click → which sale.
4. **2 pins/day is a steady drip — not a single-day blast.** Pinterest rewards consistency. Schedule everything in advance.
5. **Variety in design + hook within consistency in palette.** Same brand colors for every pin. Different headlines, layouts, and angles.

## 5. Pin design rules (locked across all 28)

- **Size:** 1000 × 1500 px (2:3 ratio).
- **Palette:** Sage `#A8C5A8` / cream `#FAF6EE` / terracotta `#D49A7C` / butter `#E8D88A` / charcoal `#3A3A3A`. Same palette every pin.
- **Type:** 1 display font (Fraunces) + 1 body font (Inter) + Caveat for handwritten accents only.
- **Headline:** 6–8 visible words max. Set in Fraunces 600 at 56–80pt.
- **Hierarchy:** Top third = headline. Middle third = visual peek of product. Bottom third = brand mark + URL.
- **No-no:** No stock-photo "perfect mom" faces, no rainbow gradients, no clipart cartoons, no busy patterns.
- **Always:** Brand URL in the bottom-right corner of every pin (small, in Inter 11pt at 60% opacity).
