## Promise (one sentence)

> The 6-week soft-launch system that turns the August panic and September meltdown cycle into a calm, predictable on-ramp into the school year — for ADHD kids ages 6–16 and the parents who love them.

## Three things the buyer gets that they can't easily get elsewhere

1. **A 6-week countdown calendar** built around ADHD neurology — sleep reset, sensory ramp-up, anchor routines, emotional re-entry — not a generic "buy supplies" checklist.
2. **A parent-advocacy section** with ready-to-send teacher emails, an accommodations menu, an IEP/504 quick-reference, and a "Meet the Teacher" introduction letter.
3. **A first-month protocol for restraint collapse** — daily decompression, weekend reset, school refusal plan, and big-feelings scripts.

## Why this kit is structurally different from a single chart

- **System, not a sheet.** 26 pages organized into 6 sections with a defined sequence.
- **Parent + kid + teacher** under one cover. Most competitors pick one audience and stop.
- **Time-aware.** 6 weeks before → first day → first month → ongoing weekly tracker. Buyers know exactly when to use each page.
- **Reusable.** Designed to laminate. Same pages used annually for grades 1–10.
