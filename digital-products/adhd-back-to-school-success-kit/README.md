## The product (one sentence)

> *The ADHD Back-to-School Success Kit gives ADHD families one calm, complete printable system for the entire school year — built for the 9pm tired parent, designed for the ADHD brain, and ready to print tonight.*

## What's in the kit (11 printables, 17 designed pages, one PDF)

1. Morning Routine Chart
2. Bedtime Routine Chart
3. Homework Planner
4. Weekly Planner (grid + reflection)
5. Backpack Checklist
6. School Supply Checklist
7. Focus Tracker
8. Reward System Chart
9. Emotion Regulation Worksheet (feelings/body + coping menu)
10. Parent–Teacher Communication Log
11. Goal-Setting Worksheet (kid + parent)

Plus Cover, How-to-Use, and a Letter to the Parent.

## Pricing (single price + optional discount only)

- **Standard: $24**
- **Launch discount: $17** (first 14 days only — coupon `EARLYBIRD`)

Floor: never publicly below $17.

## How to ship in 14 days

1. **Day -7:** Build the PDF in Canva using `01-product-definition.md`. (~12 hours over 3 sessions.)
2. **Day -2:** Publish the Payhip listing using `02-payhip-sales-page.md` verbatim. (~1 hour.)
3. **Day -1:** Schedule the first 14 days of Pinterest pins from `03-pinterest-14-day-launch.md`. (~2 hours.)
4. **Day 1:** Public launch. AM and PM pin live. Monitor Payhip dashboard.
5. **Days 1–14:** Daily 25-minute Pinterest rhythm (5 min AM pin + 5 min repin + 8 min engagement + 5 min PM pin + 2 min replies).
6. **Day 14:** Launch price closes. Standard $24 takes over. Identify top 5 pins by UTM. Re-render with 3 new visual variants each.

## What's deliberately NOT here

- ❌ No SKU 1 / SKU 2 split
- ❌ No upsell ladder

- ❌ No follow-up product roadmap (decide that AFTER first sales prove the model)

## Success metric

This system is successful if and only if it:

- ✓ Generates first sales from cold Pinterest traffic by Day 14
- ✓ Converts without needing additional products
- ✓ Functions as a single clear offer

Anything more is scope creep. Ship this first.
