# 05 — Bundle Strategy & Pricing Recommendations

**Doc purpose:** Define the product line's pricing architecture, anchor strategy, and bundle SKUs so every customer touchpoint pushes toward the highest-margin, lowest-maintenance offer.

---

## 1. Strategic principles

1. **Anchor high, sell mid.** The Mega Bundle's "regular price" sets the perceived value ceiling; the Success Kit becomes the obvious mid-tier choice.
2. **Tripwire low, ascend fast.** A $7 First-Day mini gets cold buyers in the door; an upsell flow brings them to the kit and bundle within 7 days.
3. **One product, three packages.** Same content, three wrappers (single, kit, bundle). Solo-creator-friendly.
4. **No new content for new SKUs.** Bundle SKUs are repackages of existing files. No fresh design work to maintain a 5-product catalog.
5. **Pricing rises on a schedule, not on whim.** Launch → standard → holiday → annual increase. Buyers learn the rhythm.

## 3. The Mega Bundle (P0 — must launch alongside the kit)

### 3.1 What's in the bundle

1. ADHD Back-to-School Success Kit (26pp) — $29
2. ADHD Morning Routine Toolkit — $11
3. ADHD Homework Survival Toolkit — $11
4. ADHD After-School Reset Toolkit — $11
5. ADHD Emotional Regulation Toolkit — $11

**Sum-of-parts retail:** $73
**Bundle launch price:** $39 (≈47% off — strong anchor)
**Bundle standard price:** $49 (≈33% off)
**Bundle holiday price:** $59 (≈19% off — still cheaper than the parts)

### 3.2 Bundle hero copy

**Title:** `ADHD School Year Mega Bundle — Back to School Kit + 4 ADHD Toolkits`

**Tagline:** `Every system you need for an ADHD school year — from the August soft launch to the after-school crash.`

**Hero list (use on every channel):**
- The 6-Week Soft-Launch system for back-to-school
- A morning routine that survives ADHD before coffee
- A homework protocol that ends the 4pm meltdown
- An after-school decompression flow that resets the body
- An emotional-regulation toolkit for the big-feelings days

**Closing line:** `5 toolkits. 80+ printable pages. One ADHD school year, fully covered.`

### 3.3 Bundle delivery

- A single zip file at checkout (one PDF per toolkit + a 1-page "Read Me First" navigator).
- Or a single mega-PDF with bookmarks if your platform handles it well.
- The Read Me First page tells the buyer the order to print:

> Tonight: print the Letter to the Parent and the 6-Week Calendar.
> This week: print the relevant week's pages.
> When school starts: pull the Morning, Homework, and After-School toolkits.
> When emotions spike: pull the Emotional Regulation toolkit.

### 3.4 Bundle anchor visualization (use on every listing)

> ❍ Bought separately: $73
> ❍ This bundle today: $49
> ❍ You save: **$24**

Render this as a small graphic on listing image #2 of the bundle. It's the single highest-conversion element of a bundle listing.

### 4.1 Conversion targets to plan against

(Use as starting hypotheses; adjust monthly based on actuals.)

| Step | Working target |
|---|---|
| Pin → blog click-through | 1.5–3% |
| Blog → email opt-in | 8–15% |
| Email opt-in → tripwire purchase | 5–10% |
| Tripwire → kit purchase | 15–25% |
| Kit → bundle upgrade | 8–15% |
| Bundle / kit → add-on | 5–10% |

### 4.2 Average-order-value math (working scenario)

- 100 email opt-ins/week → 7 tripwire buyers ($49 gross) → 1.4 kit buyers ($28 gross) → 0.15 bundle upgraders ($6 gross). About $83 weekly gross from a single funnel cohort, BEFORE any direct kit/bundle sales from Etsy or Pinterest organic.
- Direct Etsy / Payhip sales of the kit and bundle stack on top.
- Goal: **3 Pinterest funnels live** by August 1 (different hooks → different blog posts → same opt-in) to triple the cohort math without tripling effort.

## 6. Add-on / upsell catalog

### 6.1 Editable Canva master (+$12)

- **Pitch:** "Customize every page in your own colors, fonts, and language."
- **Placement:** Order-bump checkbox on every checkout page.
- **Take rate target:** 12–18%.

### 6.2 Therapist / Classroom license (+$25)

- **Pitch:** "Use the kit with multiple families / students under one license."
- **Placement:** Order-bump checkbox + standalone listing for B2B buyers.
- **Take rate target:** 3–6% (small, but high margin).

### 6.3 Print-shop bundle (+$0, free with kit)

- **Pitch:** "Free print-shop spec sheet — exactly which pages to print color, B&W, and laminate."
- **Placement:** Free download in the post-purchase email.
- **Why:** Reduces buyer friction; increases reviews and reuse-bundle conversions.

### 6.4 Coaching / consult (Phase 3, optional)

- **Pitch:** "60-min back-to-school strategy call for one ADHD family."
- **Pricing:** $99 intro / $149 standard.
- **Placement:** Email follow-up only; never on a public page (controls volume).

## 7. The "do nothing" package vs. the "do everything" package

A clean way to talk to indecisive buyers on Pinterest, Instagram, and email.

> **The "I just need ONE thing tonight" package** → $7 First-Day Survival Plan.
>
> **The "I want a real plan for the whole back-to-school season" package** → $29 Back-to-School Success Kit.
>
> **The "I want the entire ADHD school year handled" package** → $49 Mega Bundle.

This three-tier framing can be reused on every email, every blog CTA, and every Pinterest pin description.

---

## 8. Refund / risk-reversal policy (drives conversion at every tier)

Standard digital-product policy in this category, written like a human:

> **Our promise.** If you buy the kit and it isn't what you needed, email us within 14 days and we'll refund you. We'd rather have your trust than your $29.

Place it in the checkout page footer, the listing description, AND the post-purchase email. Refund rates in this niche are typically 1–3%; the conversion lift from the promise outweighs the cost.

---

## 9. Bundle-strategy do's and don'ts

**DO:**
- Show the sum-of-parts price on every bundle visual.
- Sell the bundle on a separate Etsy listing AND as an upsell on the kit's checkout page.
- Make the bundle the default upsell after every single-toolkit purchase.
- Use seasonal language ("School Year") so the bundle re-launches each summer with a refreshed mockup.

**DON'T:**
- Sell the bundle for less than the cost of the kit + 1 standard toolkit. (Floor: $39.)
- Discount the bundle on a flash sale below $39. Protect the anchor.
- Add a 6th product to the bundle without raising the price. Reset the anchor when adding scope.
- Offer custom bundles. Two SKUs (kit + bundle) keep this solo-creator-sustainable.
