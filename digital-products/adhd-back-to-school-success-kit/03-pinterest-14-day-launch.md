# 03 — Pinterest 14-Day Launch (28 Pins, Direct CTA to Payhip)

**Doc purpose:** The complete 14-day Pinterest plan to drive cold ADHD-parent traffic to the Payhip product page. Two pins per day. Emotional hooks. SEO keywords. Direct CTA to Payhip.

**Goal:** Generate first sales from cold Pinterest traffic in 14 days.

---

## 1. Daily rhythm (locked)

| Time | Action | Time cost |
|---|---|---|
| 10:00 AM | Schedule the AM pin | 5 min |
| 10:05 AM | Repin yesterday's top performer | 2 min |
| 10:10 AM | Engage with 5 ADHD-parenting pins (save / comment) | 8 min |
| 8:00 PM | Schedule the PM pin | 5 min |
| 8:05 PM | Reply to comments | 5 min |

**Total daily Pinterest time:** ~25 minutes. Pre-schedule the whole 14 days using Tailwind or Pinterest's native scheduler so daily effort is purely engagement.

---

## 2. Pin design rules (locked across all 28)

| Element | Spec |
|---|---|
| Size | 1000 × 1500 px (2:3 ratio) |
| Palette | Brand only: sage `#A8C5A8`, cream `#FAF6EE`, terracotta `#D49A7C`, butter `#E8D88A`, charcoal `#3A3A3A` |
| Type | Fraunces (display) + Inter (body) + Caveat (kid-facing accents only) |
| Headline | 6–8 visible words max |
| Hero zone | Top third headline · middle third visual peek · bottom third brand mark + URL |
| No-no | No stock-photo "perfect mom" faces, no rainbow gradients, no clipart cartoons |
