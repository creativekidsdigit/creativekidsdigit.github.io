# 04 — Etsy / Payhip / Shopify SEO Package

**Doc purpose:** Drop-in, copy-paste-ready listing assets for the three platforms we sell on. Each platform has different field constraints — content here is tuned to each.

> **Compliance note.** Every claim is descriptive (what the product *is*) or aspirational (what it *helps with*) — never a clinical promise. Avoid "cure," "treat," "diagnose," "guaranteed."
