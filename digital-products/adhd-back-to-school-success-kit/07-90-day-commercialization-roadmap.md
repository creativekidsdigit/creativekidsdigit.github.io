# 07 — 90-Day Commercialization Roadmap

**Doc purpose:** Day-by-day, week-by-week plan to take the ADHD Back-to-School Success Kit from raw outline to a steady-state revenue product, sustainable for a solo creator.

**Anchor date:** Day 1 = the public launch day. Plan backward from your target launch (recommended: between **mid-July and August 1** for the back-to-school surge).

**Constraint:** Solo creator. Maximum ~10 focused work hours per week. Every task is sized for that capacity.

---

## Phase 1 — Launch (Days 1–14): "Sell the kit"

### Week 1 (Days 1–7): Public launch push

**Daily rhythm (~90 min/day):**
- 30 min: post 3 new pins / engage 30 min on Pinterest / repin top performer
- 30 min: respond to email replies, Etsy convos, comments
- 30 min: 1 Instagram post or story + cross-post a snippet to email if it lands

**Specific hits this week:**
- [ ] Day 1: Send launch announcement email to full list at the published $19 price.
- [ ] Day 1: Post Instagram launch reel.
- [ ] Day 3: Send "Did the calendar make sense?" engagement email.
- [ ] Day 5: Publish blog posts 4–6.
- [ ] Day 7: First conversion ask email — "$5 off through Sunday."
- [ ] Day 7: Raise Etsy price from launch ($19) to week-2 tier ($24) at end-of-day. Update bundle to $44.

**End-of-week checkpoint:**
- 30+ kit sales target
- 100+ email opt-ins target
- 10+ saves on at least 3 of the 5 launch pins
- Refund rate <3%

### Week 2 (Days 8–14): Steady push + bundle escalation

- [ ] Continue 2–3 new pins/day; identify the top 2 hooks and create 5 more variants of each.
- [ ] Day 10: Mega Bundle math email.
- [ ] Day 12: Publish blog posts 7–8.
- [ ] Day 14: Last-call launch-price email; raise kit to $29 / bundle to $49 at end-of-day.
- [ ] Send first review request to the buyers from week 1 (wait at least 7 days post-purchase).

**End-of-week checkpoint:**
- 50+ total kit/bundle sales target
- 250+ email opt-ins
- First 1–3 Etsy reviews live
- First IG carousel of saved pins (proof / share-bait)

## Phase 3 — Sustain & Extend (Days 46–90): "Hands-off compounding"

### Week 8–9 (Days 46–59)

- [ ] Move all weekly pinning to a **once-a-month batch session** (3 hours, schedules 30 days at once).
- [ ] Move email cadence to **once a week**, mostly evergreen content from the blog.
- [ ] Decide on KDP variant: pick **one** to publish first (recommend the *ADHD Student Back-to-School Planner* — undated, 100–120pp).
- [ ] Begin KDP interior layout using the same Canva master + KDP-specific spec sheet (8.5×11, B&W interior, color cover only).
- [ ] Audit Etsy listing performance. Re-test alternative title (Variant A or B from §2.1) for 14 days if conversion is below target.

### Week 10–11 (Days 60–73)

- [ ] Finish KDP interior. Publish to KDP. Set price at $14.99.
- [ ] Re-render hero mockups with one autumn-leaning palette tweak (warmer cream, deeper sage). Refresh listing image #1 across all platforms.
- [ ] Plan the "Mid-Year Reset" pivot: same kit, repositioned for Q2 reboot in January. Draft 3 new pin angles.
- [ ] Light user research: send a 3-question survey to buyers. (What did you use most? What was missing? Would you buy again?)

### Week 12–13 (Days 74–90)

- [ ] Publish "Mid-Year Reset" blog post + 3 pins. Quietly seed Pinterest before the December–January search lift.
- [ ] Publish second Phase-2 KDP product (Parent Workbook variant) if survey demand justifies.
- [ ] Final retro for Day 90:
  - [ ] Total sales by SKU
  - [ ] Pin performance by hook
  - [ ] Email list growth + open / click rates
  - [ ] Reviews count + average rating
  - [ ] Time spent / week (aim ≤ 5 hours/week steady-state by Day 90)
- [ ] Update `00-market-research.md` and this roadmap with the lessons. Archive this season's version with date stamp.

**End of Phase 3 checkpoints:**
- Kit + bundle steady-state sales 5–15/week without daily effort
- KDP paperback live (1, possibly 2 SKUs)
- Email list: 800–1,500
- Pinterest: 80–100 unique pins, top 10 doing 80% of the saves
- One full retro doc + one updated annual research refresh ready for next year
