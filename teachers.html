What's inside every plan

16 sections. Zero filler.

Every lesson plan follows the same premium, inspector-ready structure — built on the official Competency-Based Approach.

  1. Header & identification — level, stream, unit, sequence, time
  2. Target competency — phrased as a "Can-do" statement
  3. SMART learning objectives — tagged with Bloom verbs
  4. Cross-curricular & transversal competencies
  5. Core values — identity, citizenship, openness
  6. Materials & teaching aids
  7. Anticipated difficulties with mitigation
  8. Previous knowledge assumed
  9. Lesson stages table — warm-up → production
  10. Expected pupils' answers for every prompt
  11. Differentiation strategies for 3 ability tiers
  12. Formative + summative assessment
  13. Meaningful homework
  14. Remedial activities
  15. Classroom management tips
  16. Self-reflection prompts

FAQ

Teacher questions, answered

Are these plans aligned with the official Algerian curriculum?

Yes. Every plan follows the Competency-Based Approach (APC) and references the official textbooks: At the Crossroads (1AS), Getting Through (2AS) and New Prospects (3AS).

What format do I receive?

You get an instant download with two files per plan: a print-ready PDF and a fully editable Microsoft Word (.docx) version so you can adapt the content to your class.

Can I edit the lesson plans?

Absolutely. The editable Word file is unlocked. Change names, swap activities, adjust timing — whatever fits your class profile.

Can I use these for my classroom and inspection?

Yes. The licence covers personal classroom use, including printing for your pupils and presenting the plan during an inspection visit. Resale or redistribution is not allowed.

Do you offer school-wide licences?

Yes. For department or school-wide use, contact us through the form below for a multi-teacher licence at a discounted rate.

How do I pay from Algeria?

We accept CIB and Edahabia cards, plus international cards via Payhip. Bank transfer (CCP / BaridiMob) is available on request — just reach out.

Do you take custom requests?

Yes. If you need a specific unit, sequence or remedial lesson that's not yet in the shop, send us a brief and we'll quote a custom plan within 48 hours.
