## Important guardrails

1. **Don't fake numbers.** If you haven't actually had a flood of messages
   today, edit Post 3's "Thank you to everyone..." line to something true,
   like:

   > "If you bought today: thank you. If you're still deciding: $4.99 ends
   >  at midnight tonight."

2. **Use the homepage countdown** as social proof — when you share the link,
   the page itself shows a live ticking countdown to deadline. That's stronger
   than urgency words in the post.

3. **Reddit safety reminder:** none of these posts go on Reddit unaltered.
   The one Reddit post you've already drafted (r/ADHD_parenting) is
   value-first with no link in body. Don't dilute it.

4. **WhatsApp pacing:** if you posted Post 1 in a group at 9am, don't post
   Post 2 in the **same** group at 9pm. Either rotate groups or pick a
   different hook for the same group.

5. **After T+24h:** delete the launch banner deadline OR extend it 24 more
   hours. Letting an "URGENT — ENDS TONIGHT" banner persist for a week is
   the fastest way to lose trust on your site.
